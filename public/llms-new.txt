### Primary Description
GDG Vishnu Institute of Technology is a vibrant student-led community at VIT Bhimavaram that brings together tech enthusiasts, developers, designers, and innovators. As an official Google Developer Group On Campus, we focus on leveraging cutting-edge Google technologies to empower students through hands-on workshops, hackathons, technical sessions, and networking opportunities with industry experts. The initiative connects students with peers locally and globally through the worldwide Google Developer Groups network.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### General Questions

**Q1: What is GDG Vishnu Institute of Technology?**  
A: GDG on Campus - Vishnu Institute of Technology is an official Google Developer Group community at VIT Bhimavaram. We're a student-led organization focused on empowering students with Google technologies through workshops, hackathons, and technical events. We're recognized as one of the top GDG communities in India, ranking Top 2 for GenAI Study Jams.

**Q2: Who can join GDG VITB?**  
A: All students from VIT Bhimavaram are welcome to join, regardless of their branch, year, or skill level. We welcome both technical and non-technical students who are eager to learn, grow, and contribute to the tech community.

**Q3: Is there any membership fee?**  
A: No, GDG VITB membership is completely free. All our events, workshops, and resources are provided at no cost to students.

**Q4: What are the benefits of joining GDG VITB?**  
A: Members gain access to:
- Free technical workshops and training sessions
- Networking opportunities with industry experts
- Hands-on project experience
- Certificates and recognition
- Career guidance and mentorship
- Access to Google Cloud credits and resources
- Participation in hackathons and competitions
- Leadership opportunities
- Resume building support
- Interview preparation
